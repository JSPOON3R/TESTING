Why can I see two windows?

This page displays both your screen and the screen of your guest.

In a normal session, these windows would be on two different computers

For demo purposes, we have put them side by side.

The window on the left is you. The window on the right is your guest.

How can I test the co-browsing experience?

The left page is your window and is a regular browsing session

Treat the left window like your own browser explore the web

The right window is your guest's window

Your guest can see what you are doing and annotate

What if my guest wants to take control?

To begin with you (left window) are in control of the session

Your guest can ask to take control

You can approve or reject this request

If approved, your guest will now lead navigation. You can watch and annotate.
